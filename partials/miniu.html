Mini-University is a one-day event designed to encourage underserved Bay Area high school students to pursue careers in STEM. Participants will tour academic labs, learn about STEM career paths, participate in engineering activities, hear from STEM professors and current engineering students, and much more! This semester’s event will take place on Friday, November 16, 2018, from 8 AM - 3:30 PM. Spots are limited and applications are accepted on a first-come-first-served basis, with priority given to students from underserved communities.

Mini-University is open to students of all genders. We especially encourage students from first-generation, low-income, and/or minority backgrounds to apply. This event is geared towards 9th, 10th, and 11th grade students who are considering majoring in stem, tech, engineering, or math related fields (or who simply want to learn more about what a STEM career would be like!)

Sign up here for our mailing list to be notified when the application comes out. Our application will also be posted on this website in mid-October.

If you have any questions about this program, please contact us by email at swe.miniu@gmail.com. We look forward to seeing you at Mini-U this semester!
