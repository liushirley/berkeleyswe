Society of Women Engineers is a national nonprofit organization which strives to highlight the achievements of women as leaders and engineers.






Join UC Berkeley's SWE chapter.


We're a group of engineers on campus, looking to create an inclusive community of female engineers, through a variety of social events, outreach opportunities, and professional development opportunities.
