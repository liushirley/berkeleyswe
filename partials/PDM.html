SWE Professional Development Month

About

Professional Development Month (PDM) an annual series of career workshops throughout October for students interested in expanding their professional development. Every year, the UC Berkeley Society of Women Engineers invites companies like Microsoft, PwC, and Slack to host educational seminars throughout the month. Our workshops cover a variety of topics to prepare students seeking professional experience. Events include resume workshops, internship seminars, and data analysis tutorials.
